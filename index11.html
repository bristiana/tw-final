What can you visit ?

Your gateway to Germany in 4 amazing places

01

The most impressive cathedral

The Cologne Cathedral is a Gothic masterpiece with soaring twin spires and breathtaking stained glass. A UNESCO World Heritage Site, it’s a must-see landmark in Germany, attracting millions annually.

02

The biggest beer party

The Oktoberfest in Munich is the world’s largest beer festival, featuring giant beer tents, Bavarian music, traditional outfits, and delicious food. A must-visit for anyone seeking lively celebrations and authentic German culture.

03

Iconic monument

The Brandenburg Gate in Berlin is a symbol of Germany’s unity and history. Its stunning neoclassical design and central location make it a top landmark for visitors.

04

Such a carnival !?

The German Fastnacht is a vibrant pre-Lenten carnival with colorful parades, costumes, and festive energy, drawing visitors to experience Germany’s joyful traditions.

What about german music ?

Your way into rock and roll 🤟

Band

Rammstein is a German industrial metal band formed in 1994 in Berlin. The band consists of :

  1. Lindemann – vocals
  2. Richard Z. Kruspe – lead guitar
  3. Paul Landers – rhythm guitar
  4. Oliver Riedel – bass
  5. Christoph Schneider – drums
  6. Christian Lorenz – keyboards

Rammstein is famed for their Neue Deutsche Härte "New German Hardness" style, blending heavy guitar riffs, electronic beats, and powerful vocals in German.
